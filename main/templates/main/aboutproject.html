About our Project

Welcome to our media review platform!


Our goal is to provide a space where users can discover, rate, and review both books and movies.
Whether you're into fantasy novels, gripping thrillers, or blockbuster films — this is the place for you.

We allow our users to create our shared movie and book reviews database.
On top of reviews, we're planning to also build a fully functional marketplace where users can buy, sell, or trade books and movies. It’s a community-driven hub for media lovers.

This project is developed by a 4-person team of passionate students who share a love for coding, storytelling, and cinema.
We’ve combined our skills to create a dynamic, interactive web experience.


We hope you enjoy using it as much as we enjoyed building it!
